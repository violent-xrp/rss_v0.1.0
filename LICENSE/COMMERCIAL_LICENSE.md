## How Commercial Licensing Works

There is **no default exception** to AGPLv3 obligations. A commercial license is not implied, constructive, or granted through use of the codebase. Simply using, downloading, or deploying RSS does not relieve any AGPLv3 obligation.

Commercial terms are drafted **per engagement**, in consultation with legal counsel, and granted **only by signed agreement**. The specific terms — pricing, scope, duration, support, indemnification — are negotiated individually based on the intended use and the deploying organization.

If you believe your use case requires a commercial license, the right path is to open a conversation. No offer, price, or grant is made until both parties have discussed requirements and signed an agreement.
